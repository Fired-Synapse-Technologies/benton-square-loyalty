How to Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your account and associated data, please click the button below to send an email to our support team.

Important: Before requesting account deletion, please ensure you have:

  • Cancelled any active subscriptions
  • Backed up any data you wish to keep
Request Account Deletion

You will receive a confirmation email within 2 business days.

Data Retention Policy

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will:

  • Verify your account ownership
  • Delete your account and personal information within 30 days
  • Send you a confirmation email once deletion is complete
